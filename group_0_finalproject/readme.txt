README:

To start, open the file named group_0_finalproject.
click the run button and then click the start game button.
The button in the top left corner named "M" is the mute toggle button
The button in the bottom left of the screen with a red cross is the +Attack Damage button. Pressing this button consumes 50 money and increases attack damage.
The button to the right named "S" is the weapon switch button. 


The zombies come in endless waves until the shelter dies.
If the score is higher than the high score it will be displayed on the main screen.

To play, the user may click to use their weapon and the WASD keys to move.
additionaly, the "r" key may be used to switch weapons quickly.
